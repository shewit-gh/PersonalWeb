All Movies

BRAVE: offers young audiences and fairy tale fans a rousing, funny fantasy adventure with a distaff twist and surprising depth.

MONSTERS UNIVERSITY: Offering Monsters, Inc. fans a return visit with beloved characters, Monsters University delivers funny and thoughtful family entertainment for viewers of any age.

RATATOUILLE: Fast-paced and stunningly animated, Ratatouille adds another delightfully entertaining entry -- and a rather unlikely hero -- to the Pixar canon.

COCO: Coco's rich visual pleasures are matched by a thoughtful narrative that takes a family-friendly -- and deeply affecting -- approach to questions of culture, family, life, and death.

TOY STORY 4: Heartwarming, funny, and beautifully animated, Toy Story 4 manages the unlikely feat of extending -- and perhaps concluding -- a practically perfect animated saga.

JEXI:It's hard to tell whether the lack of laughs in Jexi is a bug or a feature, but this AI rom-com is sorely in need of an OS update.

THE HUSTLE: The Hustle's stars might make an effective comedy team in a different setting, but this gender-flipped remake of a remake adds little beyond its feminine twist.

BEING FRANK: Being Frank suggests Jim Gaffigan may have a real future as a dramatic actor if only he's offered projects that don't suffer from so much wasted potential.

ISN'T IT ROMANTIC:It follows as many genre conventions as it mocks, but Isn't It Romantic is a feel-good rom-com with some satirical bite -- and a star well-suited for both.

SINK OR SWIM (LE GRAND BAIN):A talented cast and a pleasantly bittersweet spin on a familiar story help Sink or Swim finish a length or two ahead of the average feel-good comedy.

STRANGER THINGS:When a young boy disappears, his mother, a police chief and his friends must confront terrifying supernatural forces in order to get him back.

AFTER LIFE: After Tony's wife dies unexpectedly, his nice-guy persona is altered into an impulsive, devil-may-care attitude; taking his old world by storm.

UBELIEVABLE:Based on the true story of Marie, a teenager who was charged with lying about having been raped, and the two female detectives who followed the path to the truth.

EUPHORIA:A look at life for a group of high school students as they grapple with issues of drugs, sex, and violence.
